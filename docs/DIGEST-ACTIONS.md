# Taking Actions from Digest Emails

InboxIQ digest emails let you manage your inbox without opening the app!

## What are Action Links?

Each email in your daily digest includes three action buttons:

### 🗂️ Archive
Removes the email from your inbox. The email is still searchable but won't clutter your inbox.

**What happens:**
- Email removed from Gmail inbox
- Still available in "All Mail"
- Can be found via search

**When to use:** For emails you want to keep but don't need in your inbox.

### 🗑️ Delete
Moves the email to trash. It will be permanently deleted after 30 days (Gmail's standard).

**What happens:**
- Email moved to Gmail trash
- Automatically deleted after 30 days
- Can be recovered from trash before then

**When to use:** For unwanted emails, spam, or promotional content you don't need.

### ↩️ Reply
Opens Gmail in your browser with a new compose window pre-filled with the recipient.

**What happens:**
- Gmail compose window opens
- "To:" field automatically filled
- You write and send your reply in Gmail

**When to use:** For emails that need a quick response.

## How It Works

1. **Receive Your Digest**
   You'll get an email like this:
   
   ```
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
   📧 Security alert
   From: Google
   "A new sign-in on Mac..."
   
   [Archive] [Delete] [Reply]
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
   ```

2. **Click an Action**
   Tap any button to take that action.

3. **See Confirmation**
   Your browser opens briefly to show:
   ```
   ✅ Email archived successfully!
   You can close this tab.
   ```

4. **Done!**
   The action is complete. Check Gmail to verify.

## Security & Privacy

**Your actions are secure:**
- Links work for 48 hours only
- Each link works once (can't be reused)
- Links are tied to your account (can't be forwarded to others)
- We never expose your email content in the links

**What if a link doesn't work?**
- **"This link has expired"** - The link is older than 48 hours
- **"This link has already been used"** - You already clicked it
- **"Invalid action link"** - The link may be corrupted

**Can I undo an action?**
- **Archive:** Open Gmail → All Mail → move back to Inbox
- **Delete:** Open Gmail → Trash → move back to Inbox (within 30 days)
- **Reply:** Just close the compose window if you opened it by accident

## Tips

- **Archive first, delete later:** Use Archive to clean up your inbox, then review archived emails periodically to decide what to delete.
- **Links expire:** If you need to take action on an old digest email, open the InboxIQ app instead.
- **Mobile-friendly:** Action links work on mobile devices too!

## Troubleshooting

**"This link has expired"**
Links work for 48 hours. Open the InboxIQ app to take the action manually.

**"This link has already been used"**
Each action link works once. The action was already completed.

**"Invalid action link"**
The link may have been corrupted. Contact support if this persists.

**Action didn't work in Gmail**
Check your Gmail inbox/trash to verify. If the action failed, try again from the InboxIQ app.

## Privacy Notice

When you click an action link:
- We verify the link is valid and not expired
- We check that you own the email being acted upon
- We execute the action via the Gmail API using your credentials
- We mark the link as used so it can't be clicked again
- We don't store or log the email content

The action links are generated specifically for you and expire automatically. They cannot be shared or reused by others.
