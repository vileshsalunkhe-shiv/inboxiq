# InboxIQ - Comprehensive Feature List
**The Ultimate Email App: Solving Real-World Pain Points**

---

## 🎯 Core Philosophy
**Problem:** People use 3-5 different email apps/tools because no single app solves all their pain points.
**Solution:** InboxIQ combines the best features from Superhuman, Hey, Spark, SaneBox, Front, and others into one intelligent, context-aware email experience.

---

## 📊 Feature Categories

### 1. 🤖 AI-Powered Intelligence (Our Core Differentiator)

#### Smart Categorization
- **Auto-categorize emails** (Primary, Social, Promotions, Updates, etc.)
- **Custom categories** via AI training (e.g., "Client Proposals", "Vendor Invoices")
- **Multi-label support** (one email can be Work + Urgent + Client)
- **Learn from user actions** (if you move email to folder, AI learns)

#### AI Assistant Features
- **Smart Compose** - AI-powered email writing
- **Smart Reply** - Context-aware quick responses
- **Tone Adjustment** - Rewrite email in different tone (formal, casual, friendly, direct)
- **Translation** - Auto-translate incoming/outgoing emails
- **Summarization** - TL;DR for long email threads
- **Action Extraction** - "This email contains: 1 task, 1 meeting request, 1 question"

#### Intelligent Insights
- **Sender Insights** - "You respond to this person 90% of the time within 2 hours"
- **Response Time Tracking** - "Avg response time: 4 hours"
- **Email Patterns** - "You get 3x more email on Mondays"
- **Productivity Analytics** - Time spent in email, response rates, etc.

### 2. ⚡ Speed & Efficiency (Superhuman-Style)

- **Universal shortcuts** for all actions (archive, snooze, label, reply)

- **Command palette** - Cmd+K for quick actions

#### Quick Actions
- **Swipe gestures** - Archive, delete, snooze, mark read
- **Batch operations** - Select multiple, apply action

#### Performance
- **< 100ms load time** - Instant app launch

- **Background sync** - Always up-to-date without waiting
- **Push notifications** - Instant for important emails only

#### Smart Filtering

- **Remind if no reply** - Auto-remind if recipient doesn't respond
- **Follow-up reminders** - "Remind me to follow up in 3 days"

#### Bundles (Spark-style)
- **Auto-bundle newsletters** - One tap to see all newsletters
- **Bundle by sender** - Group all emails from same company

- **Pinned emails** - Keep important emails at top

## 🛠️ Technical Considerations

### Infrastructure Costs
- **AI costs:** Claude API (categorization, compose, replies)
- **Storage:** S3/CloudFront for attachments
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (user data, email metadata)
- **Backend:** FastAPI on Railway/Fly.io
- **Push notifications:** APNs (free for iOS)
- **Email sync:** IMAP/OAuth (Gmail API, MS Graph)

### Estimated Monthly Costs (per 1,000 users)
- AI: $200-400 (usage-based)
- Hosting: $50-100 (Railway/Fly.io)
- Storage: $20-50 (S3)
- Database: $30-50 (managed PostgreSQL)
- **Total: $300-600/1K users**
- **Break-even: ~30-60 paid users per 1,000 free users (3-6% conversion)**
